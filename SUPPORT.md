## 📞 Getting Help Checklist

Before asking for help, please:

1. ✅ **Read the documentation** - it covers most common questions
2. ✅ **Search existing issues** - your problem may already be solved
3. ✅ **Check Discord** - get quick help from the community
4. ✅ **Prepare details** - gather error messages, logs, environment info
5. ✅ **Be specific** - detailed questions get better answers

### What to Include When Asking for Help:

#### For Bugs:
- **Error Messages**: Complete error text
- **Steps to Reproduce**: What you did
- **Expected Behavior**: What should happen
- **Actual Behavior**: What actually happened
- **Environment**: OS, Node version, browser (if applicable)

#### For Questions:
- **Context**: What are you trying to achieve?
- **What You've Tried**: Show your research
- **Code Examples**: Relevant code snippets
- **Error Messages**: Any errors you're seeing
