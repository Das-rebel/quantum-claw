# 🌟 Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior
- **Resful Communication**: Use welcoming and inclusive language
- **Respect Different Viewpoints**: Accept that different opinions exist
- **Gracefully Accept Criticism**: Focus on improvement, not taking things personally
- **Focus on What's Best**: Do what's best for the community
- **Show Empathy**: Remember we're all human and learning

### Unacceptable Behavior
- **Harassment**: Sexualized language or imagery, unwelcome sexual attention
- **Trolling**: Deliberate provocation or inflammatory comments
- **Insults**: Personal attacks, derogatory comments, or slurs
- **Discrimination**: Any behavior based on prejudice
- **Privacy Violations**: Publishing private information without consent
- **Unprofessional Conduct**: Inappropriate behavior in professional settings

## Our Responsibilities

### Project Maintainers
- **Clarify Standards**: Clearly explain what is and isn't acceptable
- **Address Issues**: Respond to all reports of unacceptable behavior
- **Take Action**: Enforce this code of conduct consistently
- **Be Fair**: Apply rules equally to all community members

### Community Members
- **Follow Guidelines**: Adhere to this code of conduct in all interactions
- **Report Issues**: Notify maintainers of unacceptable behavior
- **Be Constructive**: Focus on solutions rather than problems
- **Help Others**: Support fellow community members

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all Quantum Claw community spaces, including:
- **GitHub**: Issues, pull requests, discussions, comments
- **Discord**: Community Discord server
- **Events**: Online and offline meetups, conferences, workshops
- **Social Media**: Official Quantum Claw social media channels

## Reporting Issues

### How to Report
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

1. **Email**: conduct@quantum-claw.dev
2. **GitHub**: Send a private message to maintainers
3. **Discord**: Direct message any moderator or maintainer

### What to Include
- **Your Contact**: How we can reach you (optional)
- **Incident Description**: What happened, when, and where
- **People Involved**: Who was involved (names/URLs if possible)
- **Evidence**: Screenshots, logs, links to relevant content
- **Additional Context**: Any other relevant information

### Response Process
- **Acknowledgment**: We'll acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
- **Investigation**: We'll investigate the report thoroughly
- **Action**: We'll take appropriate action based on findings
- **Follow-up**: We'll inform you of the outcome (if you provided contact)
- **Confidentiality**: Your privacy will be respected throughout the process

## Enforcement Actions

### Potential Consequences
- **Warning**: Formal warning about unacceptable behavior
- **Temporary Suspension**: Time-limited suspension from community spaces
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from community spaces
- **Content Removal**: Removal of inappropriate content
- **Public Statement**: Public acknowledgment of issues (when appropriate)

### Factors Considered
- **Severity**: How serious was the behavior?
- **Intent**: Was it intentional or accidental?
- **History**: Is this a repeat offense?
- **Impact**: How did it affect the community?
- **Remediation**: Is the person willing to make amends?
