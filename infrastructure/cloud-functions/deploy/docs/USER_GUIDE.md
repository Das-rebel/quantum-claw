## What's New

### The Big Change

OmniClaw 2.0 completely transforms how you interact with your personal assistant. **You no longer need to remember specific commands or intent names.** Just speak naturally, and OmniClaw understands what you want.

### Before vs After

**Before (OmniClaw 1.0):**
- "Alexa, open Spotify Intent"
- "Alexa, ask OmniClaw to Wikipedia Intent for Albert Einstein"
- "Alexa, tell OmniClaw to Kodi Intent play movie"

**After (OmniClaw 2.0):**
- "Play some jazz music"
- "Who is Albert Einstein?"
- "Play the last movie on Kodi"

### Key Improvements

✅ **No Memorization** - Just say what you want naturally
✅ **Faster** - 67% reduction in time to first action (30s → 10s)
✅ **Smarter** - Learns your preferences over time
✅ **Clearer** - Tells you what it's doing and why
✅ **Progressive** - Discovers new features as you use it

## Platform-Specific Guides

### Alexa Guide

#### Getting Started

1. **Enable the Skill**
   - Say "Alexa, enable OmniClaw"
   - Link your account if prompted

2. **Start Using**
   - Say "Alexa, open OmniClaw"
   - Or just say "Alexa, ask OmniClaw to..."

#### Voice Tips

✅ **Be Brief** - Alexa works best with short commands
✅ **Speak Clearly** - But don't worry about perfect grammar
✅ **Use Context** - Alexa remembers your last request

#### Example Interactions

> **You:** "Alexa, open OmniClaw"
>
> **Alexa:** "Good morning! I can help you play music, get answers, control your TV, send messages, and get news. Just say what you need!"

> **You:** "Play some jazz"
>
> **Alexa:** "Playing jazz music on Spotify. I'll pick up where we left off."

> **You:** "No, I meant rock"
>
> **Alexa:** "Got it! Playing rock music instead."

#### Alexa-Specific Features

- **Voice-First:** Brief, spoken responses
- **Session Memory:** Remembers context in conversation
- **Follow-Ups:** "Yes", "No", "Cancel" work for confirmations
- **Interruptions:** Say "Stop" or "Cancel" to interrupt

## Tips & Tricks

### 💡 General Tips

#### 1. Be Natural

**Good:**
- "Play some jazz music"
- "Who is Einstein?"
- "Get me the news"

**Avoid:**
- "Execute Spotify Intent with parameter jazz"
- "Query Wikipedia for Albert Einstein"
- "Retrieve news headlines"

#### 2. Use Context

OmniClaw remembers your last actions:

> **You:** "Play some music"
>
> **OmniClaw:** "Playing your road trip playlist"
>
> **You:** "Actually, play jazz instead"
>
> **OmniClaw:** "Got it! Playing jazz music"

#### 3. Correct Mistakes

If OmniClaw gets it wrong:

> **You:** "Send a message to John"
>
> **OmniClaw:** "Sending a WhatsApp message to John"
>
> **You:** "No, I meant email him"
>
> **OmniClaw:** "Understood! I'll send an email to John instead"

#### 4. Discover Features

Ask what's available:

> **You:** "What can you do?"
>
> **OmniClaw:** "I can help you with music, answers, TV control, messages, news, translation, stories, Twitter search, Reddit search, YouTube search, and academic papers. Just say what you need!"

#### 5. Use Follow-Ups

OmniClaw suggests follow-ups:

> **You:** "Play some music"
>
> **OmniClaw:** "Playing jazz music"
>
> **OmniClaw:** "💡 I can also pause, skip, or adjust volume. Want me to create a playlist based on this song?"

### 🎯 Advanced Tips

#### 1. Time-Based Requests

OmniClaw knows the time:

> **Morning (6-11am):**
> "Good morning! Want me to catch you up on the latest news?"
>
> **Evening (5-10pm):**
> "Good evening! Want me to play something or put on a show?"

#### 2. Platform Detection

OmniClaw detects from keywords:

> "Play on Spotify" → Spotify
> "Play on Kodi" → Kodi
> "Search Twitter" → Twitter
> "Search Reddit" → Reddit

#### 3. Preference Learning

OmniClaw learns your preferences:

> **Week 1:** You ask for jazz 3 times
>
> **Week 2:** OmniClaw suggests jazz as default
>
> **Week 3:** "Play some music" → Jazz plays automatically

#### 4. Feature Discovery

New features appear as you use OmniClaw:

> **First use:** 5 core capabilities shown
>
> **5+ interactions:** Advanced features suggested
>
> **10+ interactions:** All capabilities available

## FAQ

### Getting Started

**Q: Do I need to memorize commands?**
A: No! Just speak naturally. OmniClaw understands what you mean.

**Q: What if OmniClaw doesn't understand me?**
A: OmniClaw will ask for clarification. You can also rephrase your request.

**Q: Can I correct mistakes?**
A: Yes! Just say "No, I meant..." or "Actually, I want..."

**Q: Does OmniClaw learn my preferences?**
A: Yes! OmniClaw learns from your behavior over time.

### Capabilities

**Q: What can OmniClaw do?**
A: OmniClaw can play music, get answers, control your TV, send messages, get news, translate languages, tell stories, search Twitter/Reddit/YouTube, and find academic papers.

**Q: Can I add new capabilities?**
A: Not directly, but OmniClaw is constantly being updated with new features.

**Q: What services does OmniClaw support?**
A: Spotify, Wikipedia, Kodi, WhatsApp, News API, Twitter, Reddit, YouTube, Arxiv, and translation services.

### Privacy & Security

**Q: Does OmniClaw store my data?**
A: OmniClaw stores temporary session data for 24 hours. No personal data is retained.

**Q: Are my conversations private?**
A: Yes! Your conversations are private and secure.

**Q: Can I delete my data?**
A: Yes! Session data is automatically deleted after 24 hours.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: OmniClaw isn't responding**
A: Check your internet connection and try again. If problems persist, contact support.

**Q: OmniClaw misunderstood my request**
A: Just say "No, I meant..." and clarify what you want.

**Q: A feature isn't working**
A: Some features may require additional setup (e.g., Spotify account). Check the platform-specific guide.

**Q: Response is slow**
A: OmniClaw may be processing a complex request. Most responses are under 2 seconds.

### Platforms

**Q: Which platforms does OmniClaw support?**
A: Alexa, WhatsApp, and Web. More coming soon!

**Q: Can I use OmniClaw on multiple platforms?**
A: Yes! Your experience syncs across platforms.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Not yet, but you can use OmniClaw via WhatsApp or web browser on mobile.

### Advanced

**Q: Can I customize OmniClaw?**
A: Yes! OmniClaw learns your preferences automatically. More customization options coming soon.

**Q: How does A/B testing work?**
A: Some users may see different features. This helps us improve OmniClaw.

**Q: How do I provide feedback?**
A: You can rate interactions 1-5 stars or leave comments.
